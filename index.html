Our Story

Made with Passion

Thandapani Co has been a cornerstone of Little India's culinary heritage, serving the Tamil community and food lovers from around the world for over 25 years. Located at 124 Dunlop Street, our store is more than just a grocery shop — it is a living museum of aromas, spices, and authentic Indian ingredients that bring the flavours of home to your kitchen.

From premium authentic masala blends to fresh produce and specialty flours, we take pride in offering an extensive range of products that cater to traditional Indian cooking needs. Generations of families have trusted Thandapani Co for quality and consistency, making us a beloved institution in Singapore's Indian community. Step in and experience the warmth, heritage, and community spirit that defines everything we do.
